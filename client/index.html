Brian's Cool Page







This Page was inspired by my favorite anime Gun x Sword. The story takes place in a post apocalyptic world similar to our own. The main character Vann, he is a drifter cowboy with a unique sword that appears to be a gun, which he uses to summon the legendary armour out of outer space "Dann of Thursday". Vann is on a quest to avenge the killer of his wife the man with the golden claw. On his adventure he encounters others who have dealt with the evil of his organization they must unite to take down the evil once and for all.
